Cambridge University Digital Library

Many items within the Library’s collections deserve to be highlighted. This may be because of their historical importance, uniqueness, beauty, fascinating content, or perhaps their personal associations. In this special collection within the Cambridge Digital Library we will draw together books, manuscripts and other items from across our collections that are especially significant. Many of them have been displayed in Library exhibitions in the past – now they can be accessed at any time, from anywhere in the world, and browsed cover to cover.

Manchester Digital Collections

Established in 2020 the MDC is reaching out across the University to garner content that reflects the diversity of the organisation, the cultural assets it holds in trust and the research it delivers. It is a growing resource for exploring, viewing and manipulating ultra-high quality images, with a parallel display of text, audio, and even video content. The University of Manchester, through MDC, is opening its doors to the digital world.

About

The CDCP is an open-source project that underpins the digital collections at Cambridge, Manchester and Lancaster Universities. This community site is dedicated to help adopters of the platform for developers and metadata specialists.
